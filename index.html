About Us

A Place to Learn & Grow

Light Academy is a creative educational centre for children in Sofia. We believe that learning should be joyful, personal, and full of discovery.

Our small groups mean every child gets the attention they deserve. Whether your child needs help with maths, wants to write their first story, explore a role on stage, or paint their world — we have a class for them.

Founded with love and dedication to childhood education, Light Academy is a place where children feel safe, inspired, and proud of what they create.
